This is an open personal space of Elif

Founder and Head of Product of base13

Base13 is an interactive, STEM-focused learning platform that is developed for comprehensive K-8 education and employs AGI (artificial general intelligence) as its core feature. Base13 is a platform where students can learn at their own pace and are free from the drawbacks and stresses of competition and grade-based success. They instead have the freedom to grow and gain awareness while learning the fundamentals of computer science.

About base13:

  • Curated for K-8 education
  • Completely away from competition and grade-based success,
  • Gain internal and external awareness while understanding computer science concepts.
